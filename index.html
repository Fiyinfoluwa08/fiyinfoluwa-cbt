About FIYINFOLUWA CNT

FIYINFOLUWA CNT is a platform designed to help students prepare for the JAMB (Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board) exams. Our CBT platform provides a realistic test-taking experience with random questions, a timer, and the option to submit your test once completed.

How to Navigate:

  • Sign up using your email and password.
  • Start your JAMB practice test from your dashboard.
  • Track your time and answer questions as you go.
  • Submit your test once you finish.

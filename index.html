About FIYINFOLUWA CNT

FIYINFOLUWA CNT is an online Computer-Based Test (CBT) platform designed to help students prepare for the JAMB (Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board) exams. It features real JAMB questions, a built-in timer to simulate actual exam conditions, and an interactive interface for practice tests.

Key Features:

  • JAMB Practice Tests: Real, randomly selected questions for all subjects in the JAMB syllabus.
  • Timer: A 2-hour countdown timer for the full exam, with automatic time tracking for every test.
  • User Authentication: Sign-up and login functionality with email validation.
  • Dashboard: A personalized dashboard to start and complete practice tests.
  • Test Submission: Submit your test once finished and get immediate results.

Goal:

The goal of FIYINFOLUWA CNT is to offer an authentic test experience, allowing students to improve their exam readiness and time management skills. By simulating the real exam environment, the platform helps students increase their chances of success in the JAMB exams.
